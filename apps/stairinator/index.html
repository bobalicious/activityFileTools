Stairinator

Turn a stair-machine workout into a FIT activity with altitude, distance & cadence.

1 Stair machines

Define what each level means (steps per minute) and how big a step is. You can name each level.

2 Activity plan

Pick a machine and build your workout as a list of (level, minutes). Drag segments to reorder.

3 Align & preview

Upload a GPX or FIT file with heart-rate data, then slide the plan to line it up. (Optional — without a file, a record is written every 5 seconds.)

Drag the graph left/right, or type an offset. Blue = plan, red = heart rate. Shaded band = your workout window.

4 Generate & download

Produces a FIT activity with laps per segment, plus heart rate, cadence, distance and altitude.

Everything runs in your browser. Nothing is uploaded. Your machines & plans are saved on this device — back them up under Settings. · Read me
